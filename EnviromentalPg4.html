Green Things That I Personally Do

I can't always do eveything green but I make an effort to try to reuse, recycle, and compost.

Image by Clker-Free-Vector-Images from Pixabay

  • I reuse by bubble tea cups and straws as much as possible

    If I'm out of town, it's hard to do this but when I know that I will be around bubble tea, I bring my cup and straw along just in case. Some bubble tea shops will even give you a discount for reusing your cup!

  • I compost my leftover food scraps, my used tissues, and paper coffee cups

    If there is not a compost bin around me, I will often put my cup in the car and then throw it away when I get home

  • This year I'm trying to buy less cheap clothing at fast fashion stores like H&M and Forever 21

    I will get shirts if they feel like decent quality that won't fall apart imediatly in the wash.
